Welcome to the 2025 Maha Kumbh Mela, world's largest congregation of people! Join us in this grand spiritual gathering to experience the vibrant culture, devotion, and unity.

The 2025 Prayag Maha Kumbh Mela, also referred to as the 2025 Prayag Kumbh Mela, is the ongoing iteration of the Kumbh Mela, a Hindu pilgrimage festival celebrating a full orbital revolution of Jupiter around the Sun. It is scheduled from 13 January to 26 February 2025, at the Triveni Sangam in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh, India. It is the world's largest gathering, with official data counting 48.83 million people taking a dip in the river by 12 February
